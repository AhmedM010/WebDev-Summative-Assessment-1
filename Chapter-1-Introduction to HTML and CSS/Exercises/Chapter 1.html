Luqaimat Recipe

This classic luqaimat recipe is easy to make and results in delicious date syrup covered doughnuts that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon of yeast
  • Around 1 glass of warm water
  • Saffron for color
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar

Method

  1. In a large bowl, add all the listed ingredients except water.
  2. Making sure the water is blood temp (35-39°C) and add gradually.
  3. While mixing, pay attention to the texture, similar to a wet dough. You should be able to grab the batter by hand and squeeze little balls out.
    • Note: The wetter the batter, the crispier. The thicker the batter, The doughier.
  4. Proof for 1 hour.
  5. Stir and pour into a piping bag for ease of use.
  6. Over a pot of oil at 175°C, squeeze the pipeing bag and cut the appropriate size.
    • Note: Luqaimat expands quickly so cut smaller than expected size and do not shallow fry
  7. Cook for 1-2 minutes (to create a shell) and continue stirring the doughnuts for 5-10 minutes to evenly cook it.

HTML & CSS Quick Reference Guide

HTML Tags

  • <h1> to <h6>: Header tags for headings.
  • <p>: Defines a paragraph.
  • <ul>: Creates an unordered list.
  • <ol>: Creates an ordered list.
  • <li>: Creates an list item.
  • <div>: Defines a section or division.
  • <img>: Embeds an image.
  • <a>: Defines a hyperlink.
  • <code>: Displays code snippets.
  • <pre>: Displays preformatted text.

CSS Properties

  • color: Sets the text color.
  • background-color: Sets the background color.
  • font-family: Sets the font type.
  • font-size: Sets the text size.
  • margin: Sets the space outside an element.
  • padding: Sets the space inside an element.
  • border: Sets the border around an element.
  • text-align: Aligns text (left, right, center).
