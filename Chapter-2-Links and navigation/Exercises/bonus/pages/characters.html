Characters

Hiro Hamada: A 14-year-old robotics genius who becomes the leader of the superhero team.

Baymax: A healthcare companion robot created by Hiro's late brother, Tadashi.

GoGo Tomago: A speedster with a passion for technology and engineering.

Wasabi: A cautious and meticulous member of the team who specializes in laser technology.

Honey Lemon: A brilliant scientist with a cheerful personality and a knack for chemistry.

Fred: A comic book enthusiast who brings humor and creativity to the team.
